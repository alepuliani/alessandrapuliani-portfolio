ABOUT ME

MY BACKGROUND

In my life I have done different jobs, I've been a hostess, a shop assistant and a nail tech, jobs that gave me a lot but in which I always felt like something was missing.
I am a very logical and practical person. Expecially when I was working as nail technician I realized that what motivated me the most was setting a goal and finding a way to get there, so I wanted something that always gave me that feeling and that's where the world of web development came into play.
